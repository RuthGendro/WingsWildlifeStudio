Welcome to Wings Wildlife Studio!

We are located in Fall City, Washington, just 30 minutes east of Seattle. We specialize in bird taxidermy and take pride in presenting quality mounts to our customers. We offer a variety of options to create a custom piece that is unique to your bird with pose and habitat.
